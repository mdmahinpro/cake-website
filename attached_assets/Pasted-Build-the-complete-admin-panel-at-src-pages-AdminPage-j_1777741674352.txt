Password input: input-dark, show/hide toggle button inside right (Eye/EyeOff icon from react-icons).
- On submit: compare with import.meta.env.VITE_ADMIN_PASSWORD || 'admin123'
- If correct: sessionStorage.setItem('cakeauth','true'), re-render AdminPage

Logout: clears sessionStorage, reloads.
